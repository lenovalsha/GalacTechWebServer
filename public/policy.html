    • Policy

    One of our policies is that the client, when using the website, enters accurate and up-to-date information. If they do not do this, then the website will not be able to do the client’s requests properly, such as linking the email and having it be up-to-date with modern accessibilities of things such as windows 11.

    Another policy is the privacy policy, linked in accordance with the personal information FOIP (Alberta) and Federal Privacy Act. These include, but are not limited to:

    1. The individual’s name, home or business address, or home or business telephone number.
    2. The individual’s race, national or ethnic origin, color, or religious or political beliefs, or associations.
    3. The individual’s age, gender identity, marital status, or family status.
    4. An identifying number, symbol or other particular assigned to the individual
    5. The individual’s fingerprints, other biometric information, blood type, genetic information, or inheritable characteristics
    6. Information about the individual’s health and health care history, including information about a physical or mental disability
    7. Information about the individual’s educational, financial, employment or criminal history, including criminal records where a pardon has been given
    8. Anyone else’s opinion about the individual; and the individual’s personal views or opinions, except if they are about someone else

    Whenever personal information needs to be collected, the website will inform the client which information it needs and will collect the information and store it. We will have sufficient security measures to prevent data leaks, and the clients don’t need to use certain services if they don’t want their personal information to be collected
